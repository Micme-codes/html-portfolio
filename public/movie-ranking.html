My Top Four (4) Movies Of All-Time.

  • Straight Outta Compton.

    • This is an absolutely interesting movie, it's a documentary movie on this rise of the popular American music group named N.W.A (Niggaz Witz Attitude) It's an action packed and insightful movie, I highly recommend it.

  • Wu-tang Clan: An American Saga.

    • It's an intriguing series. It's a documentary series about the rise to fame of the popular American music group named Wu-tang, I highly recommend it.

  • Game Of Thrones.

    • This is an action-packed, suspenseful and highly fascinating series, I absolutely recommend it.

  • Breaking Bad.

    • This is a must-watch crime series, it's highly recommended.
